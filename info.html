The 2019 lineup

Jhene Aiko is an American singer and songwriter who has smooth R&B songs that are sure to soothe the soul. She has a mixtape that features artists like Kanye West and Gucci Mane. In 2017, Jhene Aiko released an album called Trip which landed as number 5 on the Billboard Top 200 and number 1 on the Billboard R&B and Hip-Hop Chart. Jhene Aiko is going to put on an amazing live performance that is sure to blow you away, definitely don’t miss seeing her!

Saweetie is an American-born rapper, singer, and songwriter. She’s relatively new to the music scene, but her songs have been topping the charts and played on almost every radio. Her song My Type debuted at the Billboard Top 100 at number 81 and her single Icy Girl ended up certified gold in June 2018. Saweetie is performing all the best songs you can dance and vibe too, make sure to check her out!

Joji, also known as George Kusunoki Miller, is a Japanese musician who has been topping the charts! He went from making videos at his home for his YouTue channel to performing at festivals in front of massive crowds. Joji’s debut studio album Ballads 1 topped the Billboard R&B and Hip-Hop Chart at number 1, making him the first Asian-born artist to reach number 1 at this chart. Make sure to check out Joji for good music and good times.
